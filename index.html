About Tvisha

Tvisha is an inter-college fest organised by NISER Bhubaneshwar that brings together students from various colleges to showcase their talents and skills. It is an opportunity to compete, learn, and have fun. The fest includes a range of events and activities that cater to different interests and passions. Whether you are a sports enthusiast, an artist, or a tech geek, there is something for everyone at Tvisha.

Join us for a memorable experience and make new friends along the way. We look forward to seeing you there!
